Who am I?

Bhargav Nitin Patil studying in Class XII (Science)at KV Ganeshkhind Pune. He is by heart a sports player who loves to play Rope Skipping also known as Jump Rope game. He loves to watch Cricket.

He started playing Rope Skipping/ Jump Rope when he was in Class VII. Since then represented KV Ganeshkhind, KV Mumbai region and KV Sangathan at Regional, National and SGFI level respectively.

He also has participated in Open competitions organized by Rope Skipping Association Maharashtra (RSAM) and Indian Rope Skipping Federation (IRSF) since 2 years. During July 2019, he represented Team India at Asian Continental Championship organized at Bangok, Thailand
